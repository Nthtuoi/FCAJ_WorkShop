# Summary Report: “Aws study group technical sharing”

### Event Objectives

- Introduce new knowledge regarding artificial intelligence (AI) and cloud computing.
- Highlight modern technology trends.
- Create opportunities for students to connect with industry experts.
- Gain practical insights and guidance on skill development aligned with business needs.

### Speakers

-  **Long Huynh** 
- **Ly Kien Huy** 
- **Thinh Nguyen** 
- **Thao Nguyen phuong** 

### Highlights
The event series features multiple sessions covering various topics, focusing on:

- Prompt Engineering and optimization techniques for Large Language Models (LLMs).
- Strategies for learning from the successful models of modern technology platforms.
- Growth mindset and an attitude geared towards learning and personal development in the tech industry.
- An introduction to BMAD and AI tools that support software development, such as ChatGPT, Claude, GitHub Copilot, and Cursor AI.
### Key Takeaways
Through the event, I gained a wealth of new knowledge, including:

- Understanding the principles of crafting effective prompts for AI interaction.
- Learning various techniques to optimize the quality of AI model responses.
- Gaining insight into AI application trends within software development and modern workflows.
- Recognizing the importance of a learning mindset, adaptability, and continuous technological upskilling.

### Event Experience

Participating in the **“AWS Study Group Technical Sharing”** workshop was a highly rewarding experience that gave me a comprehensive overview of application modernization using modern methods and tools. The event was lively, featuring engaging discussions and exchanges between the speakers and attendees. The content was presented in an accessible manner, blending theory with real-world examples; this made it easy for me to grasp new concepts and inspired me to delve deeper into AI and other technologies currently being adopted by businesses.

#### Practical Technical Experience


Through the presentations, I gained exposure to various technologies and tools currently used in real-world applications, such as Large Language Models (LLMs), Prompt Engineering, AI agents, and AI-powered coding assistants. This knowledge has deepened my understanding of the process of integrating AI into software development and current digital transformation trends.

#### Kết nối và trao đổi
Sự kiện tạo điều kiện để mình gặp gỡ và trao đổi với các diễn giả, sinh viên và những người có cùng mối quan tâm về AI và Cloud Computing. Qua đó, mình có thêm cơ hội mở rộng mối quan hệ, chia sẻ kinh nghiệm học tập và tìm hiểu về các định hướng nghề nghiệp trong lĩnh vực công nghệ.

#### Key Takeaways
Through this series of events, I have realized that learning in the technology sector goes beyond classroom knowledge; it requires proactively staying updated on new trends, honing critical thinking skills, and leveraging AI tools to boost productivity. Furthermore, a mindset of continuous learning and the ability to adapt to technological changes are crucial factors for individual growth in the future.
