# Summary Report: “Event Cloud Architect”

### Event Objectives

The Cloud Architect competition aims to provide an academic platform where students can reinforce their knowledge of cloud computing and cloud architecture design. Beyond testing technical expertise, the competition fosters teamwork, strategic thinking, and the ability to make decisions under time constraints.


### Key Highlights

The competition features head-to-head matchups between eight teams, each consisting of five members. Teams take turns answering questions ranging from basic to advanced levels, covering topics such as cloud computing and system architecture design. Additionally, teams can utilize special options like "Minimal Risk" and "Star of Hope," adding a strategic and exciting dimension to the contest.

### Event Experience

Participating in the competition alongside my teammates was a truly exciting and memorable experience. The atmosphere was lively and competitive, yet it also provided an opportunity for teams to network, learn from one another, and share experiences. Advancing through multiple rounds to secure Third Place was a meaningful milestone for the entire team.

#### Hands-on Technical Experience
The competition enabled me to apply the knowledge I had acquired to real-world scenarios involving cloud computing and system architecture design. Through the process of discussing and solving problems with my teammates, I gained a deeper understanding of how to analyze requirements, select appropriate solutions, and develop a system design mindset.

#### Networking and Exchange
Participating in the competition gave me the opportunity to interact with other teams and engage with the organizers on topics related to Cloud Computing. It was also a chance to network with like-minded individuals who share a passion for technology and similar academic interests.

#### Key Takeaways
The competition made me realize that technical knowledge must be complemented by teamwork, logical thinking, and the ability to make quick decisions. Winning Third Prize was the result of effective collaboration among team members and diligent preparation. This achievement serves as motivation for me to continue learning, deepen my understanding of Cloud Computing, and actively participate in future academic activities.
