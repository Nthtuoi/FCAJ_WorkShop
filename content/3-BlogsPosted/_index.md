###  [Blog 2 - Exploring Amazon Nova Forge — A Solution For Building Custom Frontier Models For Enterprises](3.2-Blog2/)
This blog provides an overview of Amazon Nova Forge, a new AWS service that helps enterprises build their own AI models based on domain-specific data without having to retrain a model from scratch at a massive cost. You will learn why approaches such as fine-tuning or building a model from zero still have many limitations, especially the risk of Catastrophic Forgetting, where a model learns new data but loses its foundational capabilities. The article also explains how Nova Forge works through available Amazon Nova checkpoints, the Data Mixing mechanism that balances internal data with general knowledge, and a practical example of how enterprises can create an AI model that deeply understands their own industry, products, and customers.
