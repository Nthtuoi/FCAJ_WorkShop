### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The internship environment feels professional yet welcoming. Program members are always ready to share their knowledge and offer support whenever I face challenges. The positive learning atmosphere makes it easy to communicate with my mentor and fellow interns. However, due to the large number of interns, opportunities to work from the office are limited.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
Throughout the program, I received dedicated guidance from my mentor and the admin team. My mentor was always available to answer questions, provide direction on problem-solving approaches, and encourage me to take the initiative in researching solutions. The admin team was also incredibly supportive, providing necessary information and materials while efficiently coordinating the program's activities.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The program content is closely aligned with the Information Technology major I am segueing. In addition to reinforcing foundational knowledge of Cloud Computing and AI, I have gained exposure to various new technologies and trends that I had not previously had the opportunity to explore at university.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
What I truly appreciate is the spirit of cooperation among the program members. Everyone is always ready to share experiences and support one another in both studies and joint activities. This makes me feel comfortable exchanging ideas and gives me the motivation to successfully complete my assigned tasks.

**5. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The program enables interns to participate in various academic activities, such as workshops and cloud computing competitions. This is an opportunity for me to broaden my knowledge, connect with industry experts, and gain practical experience beyond my classroom studies.

### Suggestions & Expectations
I hope the program will continue to expand in-depth practical sessions, projects simulating real-world business needs, and academic competitions. This will provide interns with more opportunities to apply the knowledge they have gained and enhance their practical skills.
